Quantum computing represents a fundamental shift in how processing systems compute information. While classical computers rely on binary bits (0s and 1s), quantum architecture utilizes qubits that leverage superposition and entanglement.

### Why It Matters to Global Finance

The ability to process multi-variable simulations instantly allows financial systems to map risk management cycles with unprecedented clarity:
* High-frequency arbitrage adjustments
* Predictive algorithmic parsing
* Total encryption overhaul across banking streams

As sovereign nations continue racing to build stable, error-corrected computing clusters, the global economic playground must prepare for structural algorithmic shifts.
